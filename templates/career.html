⚽ Cristiano Ronaldo Career Timeline

📅 2002–2003: Sporting CP

Debuted as a teenager. Scouted by Manchester United after a sensational performance.

📅 2003–2009: Manchester United

Won 3x Premier League, 1x UCL. Became world-class under Sir Alex Ferguson.

📅 2009–2018: Real Madrid

450 goals in 438 games. 4x UCL, 2x La Liga. Ballon d’Or ×4. Club legend.

📅 2018–2021: Juventus

2x Serie A titles. 100+ goals. Raised Serie A's global popularity.

📅 2023–Present: Al-Nassr

Brought global attention to Saudi Pro League. Won Arab Club Champions Cup.

📅 2003–Present: Portugal 🇵🇹

EURO 2016, Nations League 2019. 🇵🇹 All-time top scorer. National hero.
